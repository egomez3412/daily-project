About Us

Our Mission

At Green Plate, we believe that eating plant-based should be easy, delicious, and accessible to everyone. Our team of professional chefs craft nutrient-rich, flavorful meals using only the freshest organic ingredients.

Founded in 2018, we've grown from a small kitchen to serving thousands of customers nationwide, all while maintaining our commitment to sustainability, health, and incredible taste.

Every meal is prepared with love, packed in eco-friendly containers, and delivered in reusable or compostable packaging to minimize our environmental footprint.
