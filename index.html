Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Recipe Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl
  3. Pour batter into a greased pan
  4. Bake for 30 minutes
